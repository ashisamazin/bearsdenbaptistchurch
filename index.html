Who We Are

Bearsden Baptist Church is a welcoming community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ. We are a diverse family united by our faith, dedicated to worshiping God, growing in our relationship with Him, and serving our community with love and compassion.

When We Meet

Join us for our Sunday service at 10:30am. We also have various ministries and activities throughout the week, including youth groups, Bible studies, and community outreach programs. Everyone is welcome to join us as we grow together in faith and fellowship.
