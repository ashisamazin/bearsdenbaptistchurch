Being and Making Disciples of Jesus

We meet every Sunday at 10.30am

Our Purpose

At Bearsden Baptist Church, our Purpose is to be, and to be making, mature disciples of Jesus Christ, to the glory of The Father, through the power of The Holy Spirit at work within, and through us.

We believe we are called to be 'Salt and Light' to the world around us, by centering our lives on Jesus Christ and living obediently according to His word.

Who We Are

Bearsden Baptist Church is a welcoming community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ. We are a diverse family united by our faith, dedicated to worshiping God, growing in our relationship with Him, and serving our community with love and compassion.

When We Meet

Join us for our Sunday service at 10:30am. We also have various ministries and activities throughout the week, including youth groups, Bible studies, and community outreach programs. Everyone is welcome to join us as we grow together in faith and fellowship.

What to Expect

A friendly welcome from our stewards awaits you at our Sunday morning service, which starts at 10:30 am. Our family-friendly service features modern worship songs, prayer, and a talk based on a Bible passage. Our prayer ministry team is available after the service if you wish to speak or pray with them confidentially.

We offer groups for all ages: a crèche (ages 0–3), Kids Church (ages 3–P6), and Catalyst (P7–S6).

After the service, you can join us for tea, coffee, and biscuits in the foyer.
