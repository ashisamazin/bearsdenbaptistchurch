Giving

The Heart of Giving at Bearsden Baptist Church

At Bearsden Baptist Church we believe that giving is part of our worship to God; as a church, and as individuals, we give as an overflow of gratitude for what God has already so graciously given to us. Not only does this giving (big or small) enable us to continue with our purpose of making disciples, more importantly, it results in God being glorified.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver…12 This service that you perform is not only supplying the needs of the Lord’s people but is also overflowing in many expressions of thanks to God.” - 2 Corinthians 9:7;12
