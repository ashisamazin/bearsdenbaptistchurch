Statements of Faith

What we hold true to

→ We Believe that the Scriptures of the Old and New Testament in their original writings are fully inspire by God, and we accept them as the final authority for faith and life.

→ We Believe in the personality of the Holy Spirit. His regenerating work, and abiding presence in the true believer.

→ We Believe that all Christians are called to a life of devotion to the Lord Jesus Christ, to holiness, and service for Him.

→ We Believe that God created men and women in His own image, that mankind sinned and thereby incurred the penalty of death, physical and spiritual, and that all human beings inherit a sinful nature, which issues (in the case of those who have reached moral responsibility) in actual transgression involving personal guilt.
